# Privacy Policy — Lunar Calendar Helper

**Last updated: May 27, 2026**

Lunar Calendar Helper is built by Xiangyu Sun (sole developer). This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

---

## What Lunar Calendar Helper does with your data

### Calendar access

When you add an event, Lunar Calendar Helper writes it to your iOS Calendar using Apple's EventKit framework. Your calendar data is stored locally on your device and, if you have iCloud Calendar enabled, synced to your private iCloud account by Apple. Xiangyu Sun has no access to your calendar data.

Calendar access is only used to create events you explicitly request. The app does not read your existing calendar events.

### Subscription

Lunar Calendar Helper uses Apple's StoreKit framework to manage subscriptions. Purchase and subscription status are handled entirely by Apple. The developer receives only anonymised revenue data from App Store Connect — never your name, email, or payment details.

---

## What Lunar Calendar Helper does NOT do

- No analytics or crash reporting SDKs
- No advertising networks
- No third-party data sharing
- No tracking of any kind (declared to Apple as "No Tracking")
- No account creation or login required
- No calendar events are read — only written, with your permission
- No data sent to any server operated by the developer

---

## Data summary

| Data | Where stored | Sent to developer? |
|------|-------------|-------------------|
| Calendar events you create | iOS Calendar (local / iCloud) | No |
| Subscription status | Apple StoreKit | No (Apple handles this) |
| App preferences | UserDefaults (local) | No |

Lunar Calendar Helper does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.
